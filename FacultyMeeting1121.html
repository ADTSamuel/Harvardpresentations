Job Opening Posted 9/19/22

The Department of Physics seeks to appoint a tenure-track professor in experimental/theoretical physics. The areas of interest include: condensed matter theory; high energy experiment; precision measurement fundamental physics; and atomic, molecular, and optical physics experiment including quantum optics and quantum information. The appointment is expected to begin on July 1, 2023. The tenure-track professor will be responsible for teaching at the undergraduate and graduate levels.

Consideration of applications will begin November 15, 2022; applications will be reviewed until the position is filled.
